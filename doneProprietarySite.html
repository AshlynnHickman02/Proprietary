What is Proprietary?

Something that is Proprietary is private. It can be a proprietary software, which is a closed, non-free software for which the publisher remains with the rights of that site. A proprietary company is a private company. A proprietary company is limited on what they can and cannot do, unlike a public company. Proprietary in general is relating to an owner or ownership.
