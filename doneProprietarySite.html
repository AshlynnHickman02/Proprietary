What is Proprietary?

Something that is Proprietary is private. It can be a proprietary software, which is a closed, non-free software for which the publisher remains with the rights of that site. A proprietary company is a private company. A proprietary company is limited on what they can and cannot do, unlike a public company. Proprietary in general is relating to an owner or ownership.

Who was the first proprietary software?

DOS.Master, program for Apple II computers to put your files onto a hard drive.

How does this affect online culture?

The introduction of proprietary software early in the technology business strictly cut off anybody trying to grow a company using open-source software. Linux was one of the first open-source softwares that allowed other programs to be created and then later sold from it. Overall, the introduction of proprietary software helped the technology industry grow substantially by improving/inspiring creativity with the ability to use open-source, then change to proprietary.

Four Fun Facts
